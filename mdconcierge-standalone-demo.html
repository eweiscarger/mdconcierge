Send the referral

2
Reply once with case details
Answer the 8 questions in one reply. MDconcierge builds the case profile and selects a matched provider from the network.

Provider notified — contact locked

InjuredGuide.
Consumer leads.
Pre-qualified.

InjuredGuide is a consumer intake platform that connects injured people with attorneys in the MDconcierge network. Leads arrive pre-scored and pre-qualified — delivered straight to the MDconcierge admin portal, then routed to attorneys via referrals@mdconcierge.net.

🤕
Consumer submits on InjuredGuide.com
Alex — the intake specialist — walks them through case type, injury, employment, treatment, attorney status, and contact info. All button-driven, under 3 minutes.
⚡
Lead scored and classified
Every lead is scored 1–10 based on recency, treatment, claim status, carrier issues, and contact completeness. Disqualified leads are filtered automatically.
📥
Drops into MDconcierge admin portal
Lead appears in the admin portal with full details. Contact is locked. You review, score, and route with one click.
⚖️
Attorney receives notification
Attorney gets a structured lead notification from referrals@mdconcierge.net. Contact locked until they accept. Once they do — MDconcierge standard coordination begins.
🏥
Provider placed through MDconcierge
Attorney qualifies the client, sends the referral. MDconcierge matches the provider. Full coordination loop complete.
