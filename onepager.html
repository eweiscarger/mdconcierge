For Attorneys

  • Live visibility. A secure link shows exactly where every case stands — without a single phone call.
  • We chase the gaps. Claim info, adjuster details, records, bills — we collect and move them between parties for you.
  • One-touch demand package. When records and bills are in, pull the full package with one click.
  • Never in the dark. Patient unreachable or an appointment fell through? We flag it to you immediately.
