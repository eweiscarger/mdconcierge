Platform Overview — what's built, what's next, and where to click

The coordination flow

A referral comes in, you place it, and the engine runs the back-and-forth. You stay in the middle — connect, move, follow up, flag.

1
Referral in
Attorney email or InjuredGuide intake → a case is created automatically.
2
AI reads it
Client, firm, injury, claim details pulled out; you get an ack with a reference #.
3
You route
Pick a provider (BAA-gated). Two clicks.
4
Provider notified
Privacy-safe teaser → Accept / Decline by magic link.
5
Accept → reveal
Full patient details + one-click EHR import.
6
Schedule
Date/time picker → relayed to the attorney. Can't reach? Bell rings.
7
Coordinate
Chase missing info, move records (never stored), report UR/IME/IRE.
8
Everyone sees status
Attorney & provider track it in their portal.
